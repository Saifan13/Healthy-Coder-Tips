Support Your Community

1- Give a helping hand

Your community isn't just the people who live in your neighborhood, it's also the team of people that you work with every day.

Help to make your coding team healthy and productive by sharing your knowledge and experience.

New coders appreciate having resources that they can rely on.

Experienced coders appreciate getting insights into new languages and techniques.



2- Meet and greet

Coders are always eager to learn new things because we know that a curious coder is a healthy coder.

Meetup Groups are a great way to get out into the community to learn about new technology and new techniques that are being used in the development field.

Many Meetup Groups also welcome their members to give presentations on interesting topics.

In addition, there are also a wide variety of non-technical Meetup Groups that bring together groups that participate in everything from hiking and skiing to movies and concerts.



3- Who are you bringing along?

Is there someone that you know that could use some help getting into the Software Development field?

Many people are interested in changing careers or getting started in software development, but they don't think that they have they right skills and they may not know where to begin.

How can you help to build and strengthen the Tech Community by helping to bring talented, enthusiastic people into this exciting, fast growing field?

Volunteer to tutor.

Get involved in a Meetup Group for people who are new to coding.

Look for opportunities in communities that are underrepresented in the Tech Field and welcome them into the Tech Community.
