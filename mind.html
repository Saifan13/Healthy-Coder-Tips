Fortify Your Mind

1- Unplug for a change of scenery

As a coder, much of your life is focused on technology and sometimes you can loose track of the other amazing things that life has to offer. Disconnect so that you can reconnect to these experiences.

Let your mind take a break from technology throughout your work day.

Take a brisk walk in the fresh air without your phone in tow.

Get some sunshine.

Have a great discussion with someone face-to-face.



2- Put on a happy face

Attitude is everything when it comes to facing a challenging project. Throughout your day, be sure to include little things that make you smile so that you will always be up for the challenge.

Consistently dissipate the small amounts of stress that can otherwise build up all through your day.

Listening to a great song or having pictures of your dog around will always do the trick.

I always recommend having a bunch of interesting podcasts queued-up as well.



3- Catch some Z's

As a coder, you have to keep track of tons of moving parts throughout your code every day.

Get a good night's rest so that you will have the concentration and focus that you need to complete your projects with accuracy and creativity.

In addition to making you feel great, sleep plays a key role in maintaining a healthy nervous system so you are also able to learn new coding skills and techniques faster.
